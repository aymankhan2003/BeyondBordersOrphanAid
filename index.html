    Mission

    Mission

    Our mission is to empower orphans by providing them with the educational tools and support they need to build a brighter future.

    Vision

    We envision a world where every orphan has the chance to reach their full potential through education, support, and community care.

    About Us

    Who we are?

    Beyond Borders Orphan Aid is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting the lives of orphans in Mandera through education. Our team is committed to creating a world where every orphan has the opportunity to thrive.

    What we do?

    We focus on providing access to quality education for orphans. Our holistic approach addresses not just educational needs but also emotional and social support to ensure a well-rounded development for these children.

    Our Programs

    Educational Sponsorship

    Offering financial support for schooling, books, and educational materials.

    Mentorship

    Pairing orphans with mentors who provide guidance and support.

    Skill Development

    Organizing workshops and training sessions in various life skills and vocations.

    Wellness and Health

    Ensuring regular health check-ups and psychological support.
